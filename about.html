About Us

For fans, by fans.

PopPedia is an online pop portal made by fans. Our goal is to provide information about pop music and entertainment in a simple and accesible way.

Here you can find biographies, news, sales informations and certifications updates, weekly charts, curiosites and much more! And don't forget to check our Spotify Playlist, where you can quickly find the latest releases by the main pop artists and, of course, some good old anthems.

Want to contribute to our site? You can! Be writing articles or updating sales data, if you have the passion for pop, you are more than welcome to join us. Contact us through the e-mail provided on the side link!
